Hi there!

I recently graduated with a First Class BArch Honours Degree from the Manchester School of Architecture, which is ranked 5th globally. I am an enthusiastic designer, passionate about pushing the envelope with the relationship between technology and design. My roles at 3D PrintCity as a Teaching Assistant and at the University of Manchester as an Architectural Digital Design Assistant teaching bachelors and masters students, have significantly advanced my capabilities enabling me to further my design skills, whilst additionally practising these skills through prototyping and building a diverse range of projects as a hobby, from high-end bespoke furniture to electric vehicles and sound systems

My journey in design began in 2017 upon a work experience with Pozzoni Architecture and since, I have continuously expanded my repertoire through a deep passion for technology and design, particularly in the rapidly evolving field of additive manufacturing, such as 3D printing. Combining these practised skills with my architectural design practice through numerous building projects has further honed my abilities in design and adaptation, Computer-Aided Design, Building Information Management, digital fabrication, woodworking, metalworking and additive manufacturing

My passion for design fuels my interest and drives my commitment to mastering these skills. Each one of my projects, hobby or architectural, builds upon the last, ever increasing complexity and design development furthering my abilities every year. I am eager to further develop my skills, knowledge and career in design and Architecture as the industry continues to evolve
