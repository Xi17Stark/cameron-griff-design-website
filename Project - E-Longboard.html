The Fast Ride That Fits Anywhere

The Fast Ride That Fits Anywhere
Designing a urban transport power-house in a slim compact form
Portable Design
Engineered for convenience, this electric longboard is designed to be a compact and portable urban transport solution that seamlessly fits into backpacks or car trunks, offering ultimate convenience for life on the move
Custom Machined Parts
Built with durable 6061 aluminum parts, every CNC and hand precision-machined component delivers exceptional strength, lightweight efficiency, and a sleek, modern design
High Performance
With 34 mph top speeds, 15-degree incline mastery, and a 23-mile range, this longboard sets a new standard for electric mobility
Specs
